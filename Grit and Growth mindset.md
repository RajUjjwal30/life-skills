## Grit
- Angela Lee Duckworth explains that "grit"—a combination of long-term passion and perseverance is a better predictor of success than IQ. She suggests that fostering a "growth mindset" is a key way to build this endurance, helping individuals view failure as a temporary state rather than a permanent limitation.
  
## Growth Mindset
- This video explains that a growth mindset—the belief that skills are built through effort.
- By focusing on the process rather than just performance, individuals with this mindset embrace challenges, learn from mistakes, and use feedback to continuously improve.

## What is the Internal Locus of Control? 
- According to the video, the Internal Locus of Control is the belief that your own actions, hard work, and effort determine the outcomes in your life.
- This video is based on having an internal locus of control is the ultimate key to staying motivated.
- To build high levels of motivation, you must practice solving small problems in your life and then explicitly recognize and appreciate that it was your specific actions that solved them.

## How to build a Growth Mindset ?
The key points to build a Growth Mindset are :
- Believe in your ability to figure things out.
- Question your assumptions.
- Develop your own life curriculum.
- Honor the struggle.

## Growth Mindset as a Software Developer
My actions to develop a growth mindset are :
- I will stay with a problem till I complete it. I will not quit the problem.
- I will not write a word of code that I don’t understand.
- I will treat new concepts and situations as learning opportunities. I will never get pressurized by any concept or situation.
- I will take ownership of the projects assigned to me. Its execution, delivery and functionality is my sole responsibility.
