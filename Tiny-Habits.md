## Tiny Habits
- The most interesting idea in this video is of "Tiny Habit" formula. It states that, Make the new habit "incredibly small" because it is so easy, it doesn't require much motivation to accomplish.

## How can you use B = MAP to make making new habits easier? What are M, A and P.
B stands for Behaviour
M stands for Motivation
A stands for Ability
P stands for Prompt
so, 
Behaviour = Motivation + Ability + Prompt

My "Behaviour" towards my task depends upon my motivation to do the task, my ability to perform the task at my level best and the trigger that reminds me to act upon my task.

I can use this formula by making a purpose so powerful that I need very tiny amount of motivation and prompt but should be reliable only on the "Ability" to achieve the task.

## Why it is important to "Shine" or Celebrate after each successful completion of habit?
Celebration after each successful accomplishment of the target task is very important to accomplish more tasks and stay motivated and aligned with habits.

It gives a sense of achievement, Dopamine effect and provides brain the "Result Effect" so that our brain feels happy and it can continue perform tasks with no retardness.
