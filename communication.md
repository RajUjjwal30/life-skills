# What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

- Be fully present

- Showing that I am  listening 

- Should not interrupt between and let the speaker finish first

- Should pay full attention to the words of speaker

- avoid getting disracted by my own thoughts

- ask open ended questions

## According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? 
- Focus fully on the speaker.
- Ask questions to deepen understanding.
- Convey your understanding to the speaker  in your own words.
  
## What are the obstacles in your listening process?
- There are not any obstacles in my listening process.

## When do you switch to Passive communication style in your day to day life?
  I switch to passive style of communication in my day to day life in following scenarios :

- When the issue is not important to me like choosing which restaurant to go, which movie to watch.
- When the other person(where relationship matters) is angry, stressed.
  
## When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day to day life?
I usually never try to switch to Aggressive communication style but, in the following ways I would communicate aggressively :
- When the boundaries have been ignored repeatedly like, the other person keeps disrespecting me, if the other person doesn't respond to calm requests.

## When do you switch into Passive Aggressive (sarcasm/gossiping/taunts/silent treatment and others) communication styles in your day to day life?
Sometimes, I use this communication among friends when they arrive late.
