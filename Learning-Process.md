## What are active and diffused modes of thinking?
- Active Mode: Thoughts move quickly but stay in a very tight, familiar space. This is great for things I already know, like basic math.

- Diffuse Mode: This is like a machine where the bumpers are far apart. When relaxing, thoughts can travel much further and make connections between wildly different ideas. This is essential for solving new, difficult problems.
