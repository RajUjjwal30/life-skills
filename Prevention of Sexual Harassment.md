# Prevention Of Sexual Harassment

### What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

- Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome behavior of a sexual nature that makes a person feel uncomfortable, intimidated, humiliated, or unsafe.

##### Behaviours that cause sexual harassment :
- Commenting on women’s attributes rather than her work.
- Asking unwarranted or personal questions when women ask for leave.
- Eve Teasing
- Use of foul language with sexual implications.
- Whistling


### What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?
- Clearly tell the person that their behaviour is inappropriate.
- Save messages, emails, or screenshots as evidence.
- Report the incident
- Encourage the person affected to report the     behaviour
